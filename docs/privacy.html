Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect your data. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your tabs, your bookmarks, your browsing historyâ€”it all stays on your device. We can't see it, we don't want to see it, and we've designed the extension so it's technically impossible for us to access it.

Data Collection

ZenTree Tabs does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of your local browser. Period.

We don't have analytics. We don't track usage. We don't know how many people use this extension or how they use it. This is intentional.

Local Storage

The extension stores your preferences (like UI settings, theme choice, and tree state) locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is only accessible to the extension
  • Gets deleted if you uninstall the extension
  • Is not synced to any cloud service (unless you enable Chrome sync, which is a Chrome feature, not ours)

AI Processing

The AI tab grouping feature uses MiniLM, a neural network that runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. When you use this feature:

  • The model downloads once and is cached locally
  • All processing happens on your device
  • No tab content is sent to any server
  • The AI can't "phone home" because it has no network access

Permissions Explained

Chrome extensions require explicit permissions. Here's what we ask for and why:

tabs, tabGroups

Read tab information to build the tree hierarchy and manage tab groups

sidePanel

Display the tab tree interface in Chrome's sidebar

storage

Save your preferences locally (theme, settings, tree state)

favicon

Display website icons next to tab names

bookmarks

Access and manage your bookmarks from the extension

downloads

Export tab session data to your computer

Third Parties

ZenTree Tabs does not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics services, no advertising networks, no tracking pixels. Nothing.

The extension is open source, so you can verify this yourself: github.com/shuknuk/zentreeTabs

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We'll also notify users through the extension's update notes.

That said, our core principle won't change: your data stays on your device.

Questions?

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can:

  • Open an issue on GitHub
  • Check the troubleshooting guide
  • Read the source code (it's all public)
